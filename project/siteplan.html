Mr. Mackie Grill

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Name: Mr. Mackie Grill

This name represents a business in the food industry that specializes in grilled meat (chicken, goat, pork) and fried yam. It is locally inspired and easy to remember, helping with brand identity and recognition.

Site Purpose

The website serves as a digital storefront for Mr. Mackie Grill. It will allow customers to browse the menu, see photos of the food, view the location, and place pre-orders. The website also provides contact information and operating hours.

Scenarios

  • What grilled meat options are available and what are the prices?
  • Where is Mr. Mackie Grill located, and what time do they open?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #9E2A2B (Dark Red) — Used for headers, buttons, and highlights
  • Secondary Color: #F9F9F9 (Light Background) — Used for backgrounds and content areas

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Lobster', cursive — Used for the site title and headings
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif — Used for all body text
